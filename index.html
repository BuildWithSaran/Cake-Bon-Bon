Our Journey

Born From a Passion
for Perfect Bakes

Cake Bon Bon started with a simple dream: to bring fresh, beautifully crafted cakes and delicious café food to the people of Tiruchirappalli. Located in the heart of Puthur, we have become a trusted name for celebrations of all kinds.

From delicate wedding cakes adorned with hand-crafted sugar flowers to indulgent chocolate truffles that melt in your mouth — every product that leaves our kitchen is made with the finest ingredients, skilled hands, and genuine care.

We serve Dine-In guests, offer No-Contact Delivery, and specialise in fully Custom Cake designs. Our continental café menu has also made us a favourite hangout spot for families and young professionals in Trichy.
